Dancing rays of the morning Sun piercing through the clouds; the pleasing warmth of that first sip of tea; mellow Sun-dappled afternoons and those scenic evening walks- the charm of the Winters is like no other.

Playing host to the bustling annual festivities, the winter season has always been synonymous with happiness. Sombre in appearance, yet bright with hope- this winter, let’s reminisce and celebrate every nuance of this beautiful season.

Employing a soothing colour palette, our upcoming ‘Naadirah’ collection encapsulates the precious moments of winter. Tailored in warm winter fabrics such as handloom spun Cotton and flex Cotton, this collection features warm handcrafted Kurtas, Kurtis, Jackets and more. Showcasing smart silhouettes, comfort is of the essence.
